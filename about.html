Welcome! My name is Therese Abi-Khattar, I am currently undertaking my studies in a Bachelor of Education (Secondary)/ Bachelor Of Arts (Design and Inovation Technologies). I have Majored in Foodtechnology and Minored in Industrial (Woodwork and Metal work) also in Digital technologies. So far i have completed 3 placements at all 3 types of schools, Independant, Catholic and Public sector. These placements have allowed me to gather a vast range of teaching methods and to really see the difference in the different school sectors and how it might impact a students education.

My teaching philosophy

I believe all students can succeed when supported in an inclusive, engaging, and respectful learning environment. My teaching focuses on making learning relevant, fostering curiosity and independence, and empowering students with the skills and confidence to thrive beyond the classroom.
